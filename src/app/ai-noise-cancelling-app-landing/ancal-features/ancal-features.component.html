Canora have Several Useful Features

The app uses AI algorithms to analyze the audio input and generate anti-noise signals to cancel out unwanted background noise.

Transparent Hearing

This feature temporarily disables noise cancellation, allowing you to hear important sounds from your surroundings without removing your headphones.

Customizable Noise Profiles

Users can create and save different noise profiles for specific environments. For example, you can have a profile for commuting, another for the office, and a different one for a coffee shop.
